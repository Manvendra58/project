Returns & Refunds Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase

Return Policy

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Items must be unused, unwashed, and in their original condition with all tags and packaging intact.

Conditions for Returns

  • Items must be in original condition
  • All tags must be attached
  • Original packaging must be intact
  • Proof of purchase required

Non-Returnable Items

  • Perishable goods
  • Customized items
  • Items marked as final sale
  • Items without original packaging

Return Process

1

Initiate Return

2

Return Shipping

4

Refund Process

Receive your refund once we process the return

Refund Policy

Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return. The refund will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-7 business days
  • UPI: 2-3 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 3-5 business days
